## Prompt

```
Create a premium luxury commercial poster for a high-end hair dryer, with a clean white minimalist aesthetic, strong international beauty-tech branding, and a highly polished luxury advertising feel.

The overall image should look like a real top-tier commercial campaign for a premium beauty technology brand: elegant, modern, youthful, refined, visually striking, and instantly attractive. The color palette should be dominated by soft white, ivory, pearl, champagne gold, and light warm neutral tones. Avoid dark or heavy colors. The final result should feel clean, bright, expensive, and sophisticated.

Design a hair dryer that looks truly premium, futuristic, and iconic, not traditional or ordinary. The hair dryer should have a sculptural, innovative industrial design with a refined silhouette, smooth seamless body, luxurious matte ivory or pearl-white finish, and elegant champagne-gold metallic accents. The product should feel like a next-generation luxury beauty-tech device, inspired by premium futuristic industrial design. Avoid bulky or outdated shapes. Make it sleek, elegant, fashionable, and highly desirable. The product must immediately communicate high value and advanced design.

Include a very beautiful young female model, around 20 to 24 years old, with a fresh, youthful, elegant, luxury beauty campaign look. She should have realistic skin texture, soft glowing skin, delicate facial features, glossy lips, subtle premium makeup, healthy long flowing dark hair, and a calm confident expression. She should look refined, modern, feminine, and high-end, not mature or old-fashioned. Her pose must be natural, graceful, and stylish, with a relaxed luxury fashion-ad attitude. She is holding the hair dryer in a natural, elegant way.

The hair movement must look realistic and beautiful, with her hair softly blown in one clear direction. The airflow effect should look premium and artistic, not awkward or messy. Add elegant airflow trails made of soft luminous silk-like ribbons, refined translucent light streaks, subtle glowing curves, delicate sparkling particles, and luxurious iridescent highlights. These effects should enhance the premium feeling and visually connect the product to the flowing hair. The effects must look controlled, clean, expensive, and cinematic, not exaggerated or cheap.

The model should wear a minimal elegant white or ivory outfit, such as a modern satin or silk halter dress or luxury beauty-campaign styling. The clothing should feel clean, soft, premium, and fashionable, matching the minimal white luxury tone of the poster.

Use a bright white luxury studio background with soft gradients, subtle silky texture, light refractions, soft prism flares, elegant glow, and premium beauty-ad atmosphere. Keep the background minimal, clean, and refined, with enough negative space for typography. Add subtle high-end visual effects such as pearlescent glow, glass-like light streaks, soft reflective highlights, silky folds of light, and faint prism rainbow refractions to make the poster more attractive and luxurious without losing simplicity.

Use high-end studio beauty lighting with soft diffused highlights, gentle rim light, controlled reflections, clean shadows, and polished commercial skin lighting. The product should have beautiful reflective accents and premium material definition. The lighting should feel glossy, refined, bright, and luxurious.

Vertical premium poster composition, fashion-advertising layout, strong visual hierarchy. The model and product should be the main focus, arranged elegantly and naturally. Keep the composition balanced, premium, and very readable. The product should be prominent and clearly visible. The typography should have enough room and feel intentionally designed like a real commercial campaign.

Integrate elegant, highly visible, premium English typography into the poster. The brand name should be large, refined, and visually dominant, using a luxury editorial style with metallic gold or champagne-gold lettering. The font treatment should feel expensive, sharp, and eye-catching, with stronger emphasis and presence than a normal ad. Make the typography feel premium and memorable.

Include the following text clearly and beautifully arranged:

"AERLUXE"
"Pure Air. Pure Elegance."
"Next-Generation Hair Dryer"
"High-speed airflow | Intelligent heat control | Effortless styling"
"Designed for modern beauty"

Make sure the text is clean, readable, elegant, premium, and visually highlighted, with good hierarchy and spacing. The brand title "AERLUXE" should stand out prominently.
```
