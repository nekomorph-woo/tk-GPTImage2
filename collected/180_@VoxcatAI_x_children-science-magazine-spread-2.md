## Prompt

Create a double-page spread for a 1970s Japanese children's science adventure magazine, featuring characters exploring a swamp ruin with oversized frogs. 4:3 landscape spread.

Three-layer composition:
- Midground: Characters observing, recording, and sampling the swamp environment
- Foreground: Exploration tools scattered — notebooks, specimen jars, magnifying glass, measuring tape
- Margins: Encyclopedia-style inset panels — frog species field guide, botanical drawings, footprint charts, terrain cross-section, route map, handwritten observation notes

Style: Nostalgic Japanese adventure anime cel-shading blended with digital painting. Parchment-toned background panels, hand-drawn Japanese labels, aged paper texture. Dense informational layout mimicking a real vintage nature encyclopedia.

## 原始文本

这组挺有意思：把"1970s 日本科教冒险动画"做成了儿童科学杂志跨页。

核心不是单纯复古，而是把画面拆成三层：

角色在中景做观察 / 记录 / 采样
前景塞探险工具、笔记本、相机、标本盒
边缘做图鉴小窗、路线图、断面图、观察笔记
这样画面会天然有"少年冒险 + 自然科普 + 遗迹探索"的叙事感。
比普通角色海报更像一本真的旧杂志翻开了。
