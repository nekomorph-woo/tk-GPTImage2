## Prompt

```
[REFERENCE IMAGE REQUIRED]

Create a 9:16 ultra-premium Japanese luxury cosmetic advertisement poster based directly on the uploaded photo. The uploaded image must control the model's identity, mood, hairstyle, hair color, makeup direction, lip mood, outfit vibe, accessories, pose energy, camera angle, and overall campaign atmosphere.

Luxury editorial split layout:
left side premium product information panel,
right side dominant close-up beauty visual based on the uploaded image.

Transform the uploaded person into a refined semi-realistic Japanese beauty-campaign muse while preserving the same core identity and visual mood. Not a random model. Not a generic anime face.

Style:
prestige Japanese department-store cosmetics campaign, seasonal lipstick collection, feminine beauty magazine editorial, soft luminous skin, translucent complexion, delicate blush, elegant glossy lips, refined lashes, pearl shimmer, romantic luxury branding, expensive soft-focus beauty advertising.

Left panel includes:
serif luxury headline, premium lipstick inset photo on black leather or satin, infographic icons, short product benefits, note card element, limited edition offer box, CTA button, lipstick swatches, subtle sparkles, elegant negative space.

Color palette:
warm ivory, pearl, champagne, blush beige, rose dust, mauve nude, creamy taupe,
but adapt all colors to match the uploaded image's mood.

Ultra-detailed, polished, commercial-grade, print-quality, luxury Japanese cosmetic poster, 8k.

Negative prompt:
cheap design, cluttered layout, random face, identity drift, harsh contrast, oversaturated colors, plastic skin, distorted hands, bad anatomy, poor typography, low-end poster look.
```

## 用途

从参考图生成 9:16 日本高端化妆品广告海报，左栏产品信息面板（衬线标题 + 口红图片 + 图标 + 优惠框 + CTA + 色卡），右栏参考图驱动的半写实美妆视觉（保留原图身份/发型/妆容/氛围），日系百货商场化妆品季刊编辑风格，暖象牙/珍珠/香槟/玫瑰色调，8K 印刷品质，含详细负面提示词。
