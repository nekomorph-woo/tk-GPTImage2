## Prompt

Create a clean, minimal, editorial-style character profile card based on the uploaded portrait photo.

Use the uploaded person as the only subject. Accurately preserve the face, hairstyle, outfit, accessories, expression, pose, body proportions, lighting, color tone, and overall photo style. Cleanly cut out the person from the photo and place them as the main hero image in a balanced layout.

Design a professional English information card with a consistent, reusable template. The layout should be neat, elegant, and visually attractive, using a clear grid, refined sans-serif typography, subtle dividers, soft spacing, and a premium fashion-editorial look.

Include these sections in English:
- Character Overview
- Outfit Breakdown
- Color Palette
- Accessories & Details
- Facial Expression
- Hair & Grooming
- Pose & Body Language
- Style Tags

Keep the text concise, clear, and well-organized in short bullets or short sentences, not long paragraphs.

Extract 5–7 key colors from the photo and show them as labeled color swatches. Also include several small visual crop panels taken from the uploaded image, such as a face close-up, outfit detail, fabric texture, accessory detail, shoes or bag detail, or color detail.

The card should combine text and image elements, not text only. Keep the subject visually dominant, with supporting analysis blocks arranged around it. The overall design must feel clean, modern, soft, and high-end, suitable for social media, personal branding, fashion analysis, or character presentation.

Important:
- English text only
- Keep the original photo style consistent
- Do not invent unrealistic details
- Keep it detailed but not crowded
- Avoid messy decoration
- Maintain a stable and professional layout template
