## Prompt

```
A high-end trendy commercial beauty poster featuring an 18-year-old Chinese girl and a luxury skincare hero product: a premium glow serum in an elegant frosted glass bottle with a metallic cap. The girl has realistic facial features, natural youthful beauty, smooth black hair, luminous healthy skin, subtle refined makeup, glossy lips, soft pearlescent eye makeup, and a calm yet confident expression. She looks fashionable, modern, and premium, with a strong editorial beauty campaign presence.

The composition is a sophisticated upper-body beauty portrait combined with a clear product-focused advertising layout. The serum bottle appears prominently in the foreground or in her hand as the hero product, sharp and luxurious, with realistic glass texture, soft reflections, and premium packaging details. The model and product should feel visually connected, creating a strong commercial identity.

Style direction: luxury beauty campaign, trendy fashion editorial, premium Gen Z cosmetic branding, minimalist but highly designed layout, refined and modern, elegant visual hierarchy, strong negative space, magazine-cover-level art direction, subtle futuristic beauty aesthetic, polished and expensive look.

Background design: clean premium backdrop with a soft gradient in pearl white, blush pink, silver, and muted lavender, enhanced by translucent acrylic elements, glossy reflective surfaces, liquid glow textures, soft metallic accents, subtle floating beauty particles, and abstract light streaks. The background should feel artistic and fashion-forward, not cluttered.

Lighting: professional studio beauty lighting, soft key light, controlled rim light, delicate highlights on skin and product, gentle specular reflections, cinematic commercial lighting, luxurious soft glow, realistic shadows, depth and dimension.

Camera and composition: close-up or upper-body portrait, slightly centered composition, product clearly visible, premium poster framing, shallow depth of field, crisp focus on the face and serum bottle, high-fashion advertising composition, reserved clean space for title and slogan.

Quality and texture: ultra realistic, photorealistic beauty photography, real skin pores, natural skin texture, realistic eyes, natural lips, authentic hair strands, high-detail frosted glass bottle, premium metallic material, elegant reflections, sharp product edges, flawless but believable retouching, 4K, commercial masterpiece.

Optional poster text area with refined typography:
Headline: "GLOW BEYOND"
Subheadline: "Radiance Serum"
Tagline: "Luminous Skin. Modern Confidence."
Typography should be elegant, minimal, and high-end.
```
