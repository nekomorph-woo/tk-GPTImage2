## Prompt

Create a high-end cinematic 3-panel (triptych) fitness editorial poster in a bold black and vibrant yellow color theme inspired by modern gym branding. The design should feel like a premium fitness campaign with strong contrast, clean layout, and powerful visual hierarchy.

SUBJECT:
A beautiful, attractive, fit young woman (not based on any real person), athletic and toned body, glowing skin, confident expression, strong posture. Hair tied in a sporty ponytail. She looks powerful, focused, and energetic.

STYLING:
Premium gym wear (sports bra + shorts set) in black with yellow accents, clean white sneakers, minimal accessories like wrist wraps or fitness band.

ENVIRONMENT:
Minimal industrial concrete studio with dramatic side lighting, deep shadows, and glossy floor reflections. Add subtle gym elements like dumbbells or a vintage boombox. Background should blend black shadows with bold yellow graphic textures, paint strokes, or abstract shapes.

PANEL COMPOSITION (ALL IN ONE IMAGE):
Top Panel (Hero Shot):
The woman sitting in a relaxed but dominant pose, looking sideways confidently.
Text:
"BUILT IN SILENCE."
"PROVEN IN RESULTS."

Bottom Left Panel:
She is seated or leaning after workout, slightly sweaty, natural athletic look.
Text:
"NO SHORTCUTS."
"ONLY HARD WORK."

Bottom Right Panel:
Standing strong, powerful pose, one leg slightly raised or leaning.
Text:
"DISCIPLINE DEFINES YOU."
"SHOW UP EVERY DAY."

DESIGN STYLE:
Ultra-realistic, 4K resolution
Bold black & yellow color dominance
Modern gym poster aesthetic
Strong shadows + cinematic lighting
Clean, bold typography (white/yellow contrast)
Slight grain for cinematic feel
Balanced layout like professional Nike-style ad

高端三联画健身编辑海报生成，黑黄配色工业风健身房环境，含三面板构图（英雄照+训练后+力量站姿）+每组配标语文字，电影级侧光+深阴影+光面地板反射，适用于健身品牌广告、运动杂志封面和 Nike 风格商业海报。
