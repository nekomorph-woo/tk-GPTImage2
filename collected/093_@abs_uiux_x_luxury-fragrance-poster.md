## Prompt

```
Design a premium luxury fragrance advertisement poster from scratch for a perfume brand.

Create a high-end, visually striking promotional flyer/poster with a refined and elegant aesthetic. The design should feel modern, luxurious, and suitable for a premium fragrance brand.

Brand details:
- Brand name: [BRAND NAME]
- Tagline: [TAGLINE]
- Main product name: [HERO PRODUCT NAME]
- Product category: Fragrance / Perfume / Body Care

Design style:
- Luxury beauty advertising style
- Sophisticated and premium look
- Clean, balanced, modern layout
- Rich, elegant typography
- Dark or muted background with metallic accents (gold, silver, rose gold, or brand colors)
- Soft glow, bokeh, gradients, and subtle decorative elements
- Glossy, high-end product photography / 3D render feel
- Refined and polished composition with strong visual hierarchy

Poster layout:
1. Place the brand logo or emblem at the top.
2. Add a large brand name headline.
3. Add a stylish tagline beneath the brand name.
4. Feature one large hero perfume bottle prominently as the main focal point.
5. Include a visually attractive product section or lineup displaying multiple product variants.
6. Add a section titled "OUR PRODUCTS" or "PRODUCT RANGE".
7. Include a short list of products/services such as:
   - Roll-ons
   - Body Mists
   - Scented Oils
   - Candles
   - Air Fresheners
   - Pocket Perfumes
   - Diffusers
8. Add a clear call-to-action button such as "SHOP NOW", "EXPLORE NOW", or "DISCOVER THE COLLECTION".
9. Add contact information at the bottom:
   - Phone number: [PHONE NUMBER]
   - Address: [ADDRESS]
   - Website / Instagram / Email: [OPTIONAL]
10. Include a premium footer line such as:
    "[FEATURE 1] • [FEATURE 2] • [FEATURE 3]"

Hero product details:
- Show a luxurious perfume bottle with elegant glass, metallic cap, premium label, and rich-colored fragrance liquid
- Make the packaging look premium and realistic
- Add subtle surrounding elements that match fragrance notes, such as flowers, citrus, oud wood, amber, leaves, spices, or soft smoke effects
- Make the hero product the main attention point

Additional product lineup:
- Show multiple smaller bottles in different colors and shapes
- Keep them visually cohesive as one product family
- Each bottle can have a distinct name such as:
  [PRODUCT 1], [PRODUCT 2], [PRODUCT 3], [PRODUCT 4], [PRODUCT 5], [PRODUCT 6]
- Make them neatly aligned and elegant

Typography:
- Use elegant serif or luxury-style fonts for the main brand name
- Use clean modern fonts for supporting text
- Ensure all text is highly legible
- Maintain good spacing and alignment

Color direction:
- Use a premium palette such as black, navy, deep purple, burgundy, emerald, ivory, gold, bronze, or champagne tones
- Keep the color scheme cohesive and sophisticated

Important instructions:
- Design everything from scratch
- Do not make the layout cluttered
- Maintain a premium editorial advertising look
- Make the composition visually balanced and attractive
- Ensure the final design looks polished, luxurious, and suitable for print or social media promotion
- Render all text clearly and accurately
```
