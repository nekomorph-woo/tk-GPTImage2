4:5 vertical, 8K, UHD enhanced, zero distortion, precision grid-based layout, high-end editorial minimalism meets heat-driven culinary visualization

CORE IDEA:

Chicken tikka as a "fire system"
→ Not cooked, but transformed
→ Not sauce-based, but heat-activated
→ Flavour visualized as temperature + surface reaction

"Built by fire. Controlled by precision."

BACKGROUND:
- Warm neutral base (bone-beige → soft sand gradient)
  (#E8E1D6 → #D9CBB5)
- Subtle heat-reactive gradient:
  - center zone slightly warmer (very soft orange tint 3–5%)
  - edges neutral + calm
- Micro paper grain texture (1%)
- Soft vignette (5–6%)

GRID SYSTEM:
- Strict 12-column architectural grid
- Visible but subtle (15–20% opacity)
- Outer frame (slightly thicker stroke)
- Baseline grid (8pt rhythm)
- Coordinate system: A1–A12 / B1–B12 / C1–C12

LAYOUT STRUCTURE:

LEFT (INPUTS — RAW ENERGY):
- Chicken cuts (clean, raw)
- Yogurt marinade base
- Ginger-garlic
- Spice blend (vibrant)
- Lemon juice
- Mustard oil

CENTER (THERMAL TRANSFORMATION AXIS):
- Marination absorption
- Spice penetration
- Surface activation
- High heat exposure
- Char formation

RIGHT (FINAL OUTPUT):
- Chicken tikka plate (top-down, minimal plating)

Flow direction:
RIGHT → CENTER → LEFT (reverse engineering system)

HERO VISUAL SYSTEM:

Vertical "heat transformation stack" (center):
- Raw chicken + marinade interaction
- Spice particles dispersing (controlled)
- Coating thickening around protein
- Heat exposure stage (subtle glow)
- Char development (surface texture evolving)

IMPORTANT:
- Add very subtle heat distortion lines (barely visible)
- Micro ember particles (extremely minimal)
- Clear separation between stages
- Perfect vertical alignment

Final dish:
- Rich orange tikka
- Defined char marks (blackened edges)
- Slight oil sheen
- Clean plating, no clutter

GRAPHIC ELEMENTS:
- Thin structural grid lines
- Measurement ticks
- Precision alignment markers
- Circular nodes on transformation axis

Label examples:
"MARINATION ABSORPTION"
"SPICE PENETRATION"
"THERMAL EXPOSURE"
"CHAR DEVELOPMENT INDEX"
"SMOKE DEPTH: HIGH"

- One sharp horizontal system line (heat axis)

- Bold industrial sans
- Slightly tighter tracking
- Off-white (not pure white)

Subheadline: "Precision in every degree"
