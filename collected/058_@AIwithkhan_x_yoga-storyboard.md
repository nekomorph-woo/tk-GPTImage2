## Prompt

Create a 16-step morning yoga/stretch routine storyboard featuring a young woman in a serene outdoor garden during golden hour. Soft natural sunlight, lush greenery, and a calm wellness aesthetic.
STYLE & VISUALS:
Cinematic, soft lighting with warm tones
Clean wellness influencer aesthetic
Minimal, elegant composition
Outfit: matching pastel activewear (sports bra + leggings)
Hair tied in a neat bun
Barefoot on grass
Background: blurred garden with plants and sunlight rays
FORMAT:
4x4 grid layout (16 panels)
Each panel shows a different step of the routine
Include minimal text labels (step number + exercise name)
Add subtle directional arrows for movement
SEQUENCE:
Standing relaxed (deep breathing)
Neck rolls
Shoulder stretch (cross-body)
Arm stretch overhead
Side bend
Forward fold
Half lift
Gentle twist
Quad stretch
Calf stretch (lunge)
Light squat
Forward reach (lunge stretch)
Balance stretch (one leg hold)
Slow breathing pose (hands to chest)
Arms open (morning stretch)
Final calm pose (hands in prayer)
MOOD:
Calm, refreshing, mindful morning energy
Focus on fluid movement and posture clarity
Peaceful, slow lifestyle vibe
CAMERA FEEL (if video):
Slow, stable shots
Slight natural motion (gentle breeze, light movement)
Optional slow-motion transitions between poses

## 用途

生成 16 步晨间瑜伽拉伸动作故事板，4x4 网格布局，每个面板展示一个瑜伽动作步骤，户外花园金色光线场景，柔和电影感美学。
