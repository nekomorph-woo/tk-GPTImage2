A realistic low-light smartphone restaurant portrait of a young adult woman sitting inside a warm cafe or restaurant at night. The moment should feel like someone sitting across the table casually took the photo, natural but lightly posed.

The hair has a soft dark sheen under warm low light, with slightly blurred edges. Loose strands rest near the cheeks and neck, adding casual restaurant realism. Preserve her soft oval face, delicate features, large eyes, natural nose bridge, and gentle lip shape. Makeup follows a warm East Asian-inspired restaurant selfie style: translucent base, natural straight brows, thin eyeliner, soft under-eye detail, light pink blush, and rose-pink or muted bean-pink lips. Keep it low-contrast, clean, and soft, without heavy makeup or commercial retouching.

She wears a white thin-strap camisole top with lace trim, a small bow at the neckline, and tiny floral or dotted details, layered with a warm cream or ivory loose knit cardigan. The cardigan has visible vertical or open-knit texture and slips slightly off the shoulder, revealing the thin strap. The fabric should feel soft, warm, and textured; the cardigan shows fuzzy knit detail under warm light, while the camisole looks lighter and more delicate.
