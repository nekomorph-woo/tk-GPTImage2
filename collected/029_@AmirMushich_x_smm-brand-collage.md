## Prompt

```
[BRAND NAME = CHANGE TO YOUR BRAND].
Act as a Social Media Art Director and Digital Collage Artist specializing in bold, youth-oriented brand content for Instagram and digital campaigns.

PHASE 1: CONCEPTUAL FRAMEWORK
Create a dynamic digital collage that merges fashion photography with graphic design chaos. Anti-polished: torn edges, layered textures, hand-drawn elements, and bold color blocking that screams confidence and movement.

PHASE 2: MODEL & PHOTOGRAPHY
- Subject: One model (diverse casting, age 18-30) in a dynamic, confident pose
- Pose Energy: 80% attitude, 20% natural – sitting, jumping, mid-motion, or power stance
- Outfit: Street style/athleisure aligned with [BRAND NAME]
- Hero Product: Feature 1 signature product prominently – visual anchor
- Photography: Editorial fashion cutout, model extracted from background with clean edges
- Camera: Slight low angle (hero perspective), Full body or 3/4 body

PHASE 3: COLOR BLOCKING FOUNDATION
- Primary Color Blob: Large organic shape (40-60%) in bold saturated brand color
- Shape: Irregular, hand-painted aesthetic – think Photoshop brush strokes or torn paper
- Texture: Visible brush strokes, grain, subtle noise (15-25% opacity)

PHASE 4: GRAPHIC ELEMENTS LAYER
3-5 abstract graphic elements scattered asymmetrically (top-left, bottom-right zones primarily). Mix small (5%) and medium (15%) elements. Analog/handmade feel.

PHASE 5: TYPOGRAPHY INTEGRATION
- Brand Logo: Clean, upper-left or upper-right quadrant (10-15% width)
- Slogan in bold brush/stencil lettering or graffiti tag style
- Mix aligned and slightly rotated text (2-5° angles) for dynamic energy

PHASE 6: TEXTURE & BACKGROUND
- Base: Off-white or light gray textured background (RGB 245-250)
- Texture options (autonomous): subtle paper grain, canvas weave, noise, halftone

PHASE 7: COMPOSITION RULES
- Asymmetric balance, model off-center, graphic elements counter-balance
- 15-20% negative space, diagonal flow (top-left to bottom-right)
- Focal Point: Model + hero product = 60% visual weight

PHASE 8: BRAND INTELLIGENCE (AUTONOMOUS)
Adapt composition based on brand personality:
- Streetwear/Sportswear (Nike, Adidas, Supreme)
- Luxury Streetwear (Balenciaga, Off-White, Gucci)
- Beauty/Lifestyle (Glossier, Fenty, Skims)
- Tech/Modern (Apple, Tesla, Beats)

PHASE 9: SOCIAL MEDIA FOOTER
Bottom strip with social media handles in three-column grid.

TECHNICAL SPECS:
- Aspect Ratio: 4:5 or 1:1
- Resolution: 2400x3000px minimum
- Color Mode: sRGB, vibrant saturation
- Style Reference: Nike social campaigns, Spotify wrapped, Gen Z Instagram, Hypebeast
- Mood: Confident, energetic, youthful, authentic chaos, anti-corporate polish
```

## 用途

生成品牌社交媒体 SMM 设计数字拼贴海报，9 阶段完整设计流程（概念框架→模特摄影→色块基础→图形元素→排版→纹理→构图→品牌适配→社媒页脚），4:5 或 1:1 比例，Nike/Spotify 风格，品牌名可替换模板。
