## Prompt

```
Design Concept: The Crumple Chair

Core Philosophy: Translating the "controlled chaos" of a tossed paper ball into a sculptural, high-comfort seating experience.

Stage 1: Observation & Morphological Analysis
- Crease Mapping: Identify the primary "valley" and "ridge" lines. These represent potential structural ribs or seams in the chair.
- Faceted Planes: Break down the sphere into a series of non-uniform polygons. Each flat surface of the paper becomes a potential panel for the chair's upholstery or shell.
- Shadow Study: Analyze how the "tossed" form creates deep recesses. These natural pockets guide where the user's weight will be cradled.

Stage 2: Iterative Form Exploration
- Subtractive Sculpting: Imagine the paper ball as a solid mass. Use Boolean operations to "carve out" a seating cavity.
- Tension Simulation: Use 3D software to simulate a flat sheet of material being compressed.
- The "Toss" Logic: Experiment with gravity-based simulation dropping a digital mesh to see how it settles naturally.

Stage 3: Ergonomic Translation & Blueprinting
- The Comfort Core: Overlay a standard ergonomic template (Seating Angle: 105°–110°). Adjust internal "folds" to provide lumbar support and pressure relief.
- Blueprint Generation: Create technical orthographic views (Front, Side, Top). Seat Height: 450mm, Total Width: 850mm.

Stage 4: Structural Integration & Scaling
- The Skeleton: Hidden internal frame (CNC-bent steel rods or 3D-printed lattice).
- Material Selection:
  - Option A (High-End): Faceted, cast aluminum with white powder coat.
  - Option B (Soft): Vacuum-formed recycled plastic shell covered in "memory-fold" technical fabric.

Stage 5: Final Prototyping & Material Finish
- Textural Replication: Matte, slightly porous finish to mimic the tactile feel of heavy-bond paper.
- Lighting Contrast: Directional studio lighting to emphasize "tossed" shadows.

Design Tip: Avoid symmetry. The most compelling aspect of a crumpled paper ball is its unique irregularity.
```

## 用途

生成揉纸团概念椅的设计方案，5 阶段完整设计流程（形态分析→迭代探索→人体工学→结构整合→最终原型），含尺寸参数和材料方案，将揉纸的"受控混乱"转化为雕塑感座椅。
