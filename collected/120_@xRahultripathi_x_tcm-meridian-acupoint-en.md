Five Shu Points distribution rules:
Jing-Well points at nail corners,
Ying-Spring & Shu-Stream on palms/soles,
Jing-River & Yuan-Source at wrists/ankles,
He-Sea at elbows/knees.

Special points:

Spacing between adjacent points must follow proportional body-inch (cun) measurement — no compression allowed.

Acupoint labeling (must list sequentially):
List all points strictly in meridian order, format: Point Name [Attribute]
Leader lines must originate from the center of each node, no crossing allowed.
Labels arranged alternately left and right following meridian direction.

Negative anatomical constraints:
No acupoints placed on solid bone surfaces,
No meridian lines crossing Yin-Yang boundaries,
No merging of adjacent points into a single node,
No misalignment between labels and their points,
No point deviating more than 0.5 cun from anatomical landmark.

Background:
Warm parchment texture with subtle ink-wash aesthetics.

Title bar:
Top-left or top-right vertical layout,
Gray-blue background with white text: "[Full Meridian Name]"

Quality:
