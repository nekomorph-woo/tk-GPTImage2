## Prompt

```
Step 1:
Analyze the uploaded image and preserve the original subject, composition, and lighting exactly as is, do not alter the identity, proportions, or structure of the main subject, add playful hand-drawn doodles that interact naturally with the subject such as small characters, smiley faces, or tiny companions sitting, hugging, or reacting to the subject, doodles should follow the shape and perspective of the subject, clean thin line style, slightly imperfect hand-drawn look, minimal but expressive.

Step 2:
Analyze the uploaded image and preserve the original subject, composition, and lighting, do not alter the subject identity, add multiple layers of playful handwritten text, symbols, and emojis around the subject, organize them in clear hierarchy (main phrase larger, supporting words smaller), follow the natural composition flow, use soft colorful tones, include doodle icons (hearts, stars, sparkles), keep spacing intentional and balanced, avoid overlapping the subject, overall look full but clean.

Step 3:
Analyze the uploaded image and preserve everything original, add imaginative doodles (stars, clouds, surreal elements) interacting with the subject, include a short phrase that feels like a mini story or feeling (3–5 words), place text in a natural empty space within the image, style slightly playful handwritten or soft brush, align text with scene perspective, keep balance and avoid clutter.

Step 4:
Analyze the uploaded image and preserve the original subject, composition, and lighting, do not modify the subject itself, add hand-drawn outline doodles that trace and exaggerate the edges, curves, or pose of the subject, include motion lines, sketchy highlights, or double outlines to create energy and emphasis, doodles should feel dynamic but not messy, use consistent line weight, keep everything clean and visually balanced.
```

## 用途

4 步 image-edit prompt 在原图上叠加手绘涂鸦装饰，分别实现：(1) 互动小角色/笑脸涂鸦、(2) 手写文字+符号+emoji 层叠、(3) 超现实元素+短句故事、(4) 轮廓描边夸张+动感线条。保持原图完全不变，干净细线手绘风格。
