Takoyaki Japanese Street Food – illustrated zine-style infographic on a textured cream paper background.

Style: Hand-drawn, flat color illustration with thin black outlines. Slightly wonky and charming, like a DIY zine or risograph print. Warm color palette: golden browns, creamy yellows, soft pinks, and fresh greens.
