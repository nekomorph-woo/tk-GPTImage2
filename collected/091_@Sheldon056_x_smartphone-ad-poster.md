## Prompt

```
Create a premium Apple-style smartphone advertisement poster in 4:5 vertical format, ultra-high resolution, with a clean, modern, global campaign aesthetic.

Scene & Composition
A minimal white studio background with a large rounded rectangular orange gradient panel placed behind the subject, slightly off-center. The gradient should feel soft and luminous, not flat.

In the background, integrate oversized typography:
"PRO"
very large, partially cropped by frame edges
deep orange tone, slightly darker than the background panel
soft shadow and subtle depth, not overly graphic

Subject (Male Model)
A stylish young male model (based on reference):
light brown textured hair
short beard
round black glasses

Expression & Pose:
calm, confident, neutral expression
direct eye contact
shot from a slightly low angle
one hand extended toward the camera holding the phone
body slightly angled for depth

Outfit:
deep maroon jacket
clean off-white t-shirt
minimal accessories (subtle ring/chain)

Product (Hero Focus)
iPhone 17 Pro – Titanium Orange finish
placed in extreme foreground using forced perspective
slightly tilted to showcase camera depth

Details:
ultra-realistic triple camera lenses
accurate reflections and glass depth
brushed titanium edges with subtle highlights
soft Apple logo reflection
fine material texture (no plastic feel)

Lighting
Keep it soft and premium:
key light from front-left (clean skin tones, smooth highlights)
warm rim light from right (defines edges of phone and face)
subtle orange bounce from background
soft shadows, no harsh contrast

UI & Graphic Elements
small Apple logo (top left)
"Built for Apple Intelligence." (top right, thin font)
floating glass-style pill:
"iPhone 17 Pro"
2–3 small sparkle accents
very subtle grain for realism

Bottom Section (Features)
Four rounded glass cards with minimal icons:
A19 Pro Chip — Unstoppable performance
48MP Pro Camera — Total creative control
4K Dolby Vision — Cinematic in every frame
Apple Intelligence — Personal. Private. Powerful

Bottom Right Text
Small clean paragraph:
iPhone 17 Pro. Uncompromising power.
A19 Pro chip. Advanced camera system.
Designed for what's next.

Camera & Render Feel
50mm lens look
shallow depth of field
slight natural lens distortion
HDR lighting
ultra-real textures (skin, metal, glass)

Overall Direction
Make it feel like:
shot in a real studio
not over-designed
premium but effortless
Nothing should look forced or overly perfect.
```
