## Prompt

```
Create a premium, clean, high-quality restaurant food promotional poster in 4:5 aspect ratio (Instagram portrait post — 1080x1350) with a soft warm beige background and elegant modern layout. The main focus should be a large, realistic, mouthwatering, professionally styled image of [FOOD NAME] placed at the center, looking fresh, rich, vibrant, and highly appetizing like luxury food photography.

The food must look exactly like the authentic real-life version of [FOOD NAME], especially if it is a local dish (for example Nigerian dishes like Jollof Rice, Fried Rice, Seafood Okra, etc.).

Do not make the food look generic, foreign-looking, or unrealistic.
It must have the correct ingredients, proper local appearance, and true texture of the actual dish.

At the top center, place a stylish restaurant logo and brand name with a short catchy tagline underneath.
Use bold large headline text for the food name in the center above the dish.
On the LEFT side, create an INGREDIENTS section with small realistic ingredient images and short labels/descriptions related to the food (for example: vegetables, proteins, spices, sauces, etc.) connected neatly with curved arrows pointing toward the main dish.

On the RIGHT side, create a NUTRITIONAL BENEFITS section with clean circular icons and short benefit descriptions (example: high protein, rich in vitamins, energy boost, rich in fiber, satisfying meal, etc.).
At the bottom center, add a bold stylish PRICE section with a strong brush-stroke style background showing the price clearly in the currency, followed by PER SERVING

At the very bottom, include elegant social media icons and a social handle.
Design style should be premium, realistic, polished, luxurious, modern restaurant branding, clean spacing, balanced composition, beautiful typography, visually appealing, professional food advertisement, highly realistic food photography, sharp details, rich colors, and strong commercial poster quality.

IMPORTANT:
Avoid ugly AI-looking food.
Make the food beautiful, premium, authentic, realistic, and believable.
The dish must look exactly like the real local version of the food, not an incorrect AI interpretation.
```

## 用途

生成高端餐厅食品宣传海报（Instagram 4:5 竖版），暖米色背景，中央大尺寸美食摄影居中展示，左侧食材成分区配箭头指向菜品，右侧营养成分圆形图标，底部手绘笔触价格区，顶部品牌 logo 和标语。
