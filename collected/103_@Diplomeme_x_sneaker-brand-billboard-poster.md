## Prompt

```
Hyper-realistic cinematic product advertising billboard poster for Nike Air Zoom, focusing on speed and forward momentum.

Scene: shoe mid-air with water displacement, angled forward aggressively.

Environment: wet asphalt fading into dark gradient.

Subject: shoe in 3/4 dynamic angle, dominant foreground.

Composition: low-angle, strong diagonal, large negative space top-right.

Typography:
top left — Nike logo, small, tight spacing, subtle presence

center-right (hero) — "AIR ZOOM"
→ ultra bold, condensed sans-serif
→ very tight kerning
→ large scale occupying ~35–40% width

below headline — "RUN FASTER."
→ medium weight
→ +20 tracking
→ significantly smaller, acting as breath space

bottom left —
"RESPONSIVE CUSHION · LIGHTWEIGHT MESH · SPEED ENGINEERED"
→ small caps
→ +60 tracking
→ low contrast placement

bottom right — "http://nike.com"
→ minimal size, quiet anchor

left vertical —
"RUNNING / 2026 / PERFORMANCE"
→ rotated, very small, +80 tracking

Typography behavior: clear hierarchy (hero > support > specs > metadata), no crowding, strong negative space.

Lighting: sharp side highlight, rim light edge.

Atmosphere: controlled water droplets.

Shot on ARRI Alexa Mini LF, 35mm anamorphic, HDR
```

*(模板还包含 Adidas Ultraboost、Puma Velocity Nitro、New Balance 990v6 三个变体，结构与 Nike 相同，仅调整品牌标识、排版风格和灯光氛围)*

## 用途

生成超写实电影级运动鞋品牌广告海报模板（Nike/Adidas/Puma/New Balance 四品牌系列），低角度动态构图 + 大负空间排版系统，分层排版规范：品牌 logo → 英雄标题（ultra bold/condensed）→ 标语 → 参数行 → 元数据，ARRI Alexa Mini LF 拍摄参数标注，湿沥青/混凝土/暖色渐变等差异化环境，35mm/50mm/85mm 镜头区分。
