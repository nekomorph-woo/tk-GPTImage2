## Prompt

```
Subject & Composition:
A high-end fashion studio portrait of a confident woman wearing a premium athletic matching set. She is posed from the waist up, slightly angled to the side while looking off-camera. She has her hands tucked into the front kangaroo pocket of her hoodie.

Outfit Details:
Top: An oversized color-blocked hoodie featuring a dynamic "V" or hourglass-shaped panel design. The colors include deep royal purple, lavender, and heather gray. The fabric is a high-quality heavyweight fleece with visible soft texture.
Bottom: Matching athletic shorts with the same curved color-blocking pattern in purple, lavender, and gray.
Branding: A small, minimalist athletic logo centered on the chest in a subtle pink or light lavender embroidery.

Model Styling:
Appearance: Light-brown skin tone, youthful features, and a natural, "no-makeup" makeup look.
Hair: Styled in a high, textured messy bun with soft, curly flyaways framing the forehead and ears.
Accessories: Simple, small gold hoop earrings and a delicate thin gold chain necklace.

Technical Specifications:
Lighting: Professional studio lighting; soft, even butterfly lighting to minimize harsh shadows while emphasizing garment texture.
Background: Clean, solid light-gray seamless paper backdrop with a subtle floor-to-wall gradient.
Camera Settings: Shot on an 85mm lens, f/4.0 aperture for sharp focus across the entire outfit, ultra-high resolution, 8K, hyper-realistic skin and fabric textures.

Aesthetic: Clean, commercial e-commerce photography, high-fashion editorial, minimalist and modern.
```
