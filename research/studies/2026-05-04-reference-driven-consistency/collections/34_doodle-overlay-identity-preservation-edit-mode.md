## Prompt

```
4-step doodle overlay transformation:

Step 1: Start with the original photo completely untouched — keep the person, face, pose, lighting, and background exactly as uploaded.

Step 2: Add thin, hand-drawn doodle outlines tracing around the person and key objects — soft sketchy lines in white ink with slight imperfections, like a real pencil sketch overlay.

Step 3: Fill in decorative hand-drawn elements around the composition: small stars, hearts, spirals, arrows, squiggles, and sparkle marks — all in a mix of white and soft pink ink, scattered naturally but balanced.

Step 4: Add short handwritten-style annotations and labels near key elements of the photo — pointing to the outfit, accessories, or mood — using a casual, fun handwriting style. Include a small title or caption at the top.

Overall vibe: playful scrapbook aesthetic, feminine energy, fun and light. The original photo must remain 100% visible and unaltered underneath. All doodle and text layers should feel hand-drawn and slightly imperfect, not digital or sterile.
```

## 备注

这个 prompt 是 #12 的变体——同样使用编辑模式进行身份保留，但使用了更结构化的 4 步骤方式。关键技巧：(1) "Step 1: Start with the original photo completely untouched"——第一步就声明原图不变；(2) 分步描述让模型更清晰地理解"在不变的基础上添加"的逻辑；(3) "The original photo must remain 100% visible and unaltered underneath"——在末尾重申身份保留要求。这种"步骤化"的编辑指令比单段长文本更容易让模型准确执行。
