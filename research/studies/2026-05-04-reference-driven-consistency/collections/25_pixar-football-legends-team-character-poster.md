## Prompt

```
"Pixar 3D CGI animated movie poster style. The subject is [TEAM]. Automatically select the 6 most iconic and legendary players in [TEAM]'s all-time history — the faces every fan would instantly recognize. Render each as a Pixar-style 3D animated character with exaggerated expressive faces, smooth stylized skin, big eyes, chunky proportions, and subtly recognizable facial features matching the real players. Dramatic layered composition: the single most iconic player front and center, others fanned behind in dynamic poses. Background automatically chosen to match [TEAM]'s iconic colors and visual identity. Stadium atmosphere in the background, slightly blurred. Bold title text at the bottom: 'TEAM — ALL TIME LEGENDS' in a cinematic font. Cinematic lighting, rim light on each character, deep shadows. 2:3 vertical aspect ratio. High detail, polished Pixar feature film aesthetic."
```

## 备注

这是一个多角色模板 prompt，使用 [TEAM] 占位符可应用于任意球队。关键一致性技巧：(1) "subtly recognizable facial features matching the real players"——在风格化处理的同时保留可识别性；(2) 让模型"automatically select"最标志性球员，利用模型的内部知识；(3) "Background automatically chosen to match [TEAM]'s iconic colors"——球队配色作为额外的身份锚点。该 prompt 展示了在风格化（Pixar 3D）处理中保持角色可识别性的挑战和解决方案。
