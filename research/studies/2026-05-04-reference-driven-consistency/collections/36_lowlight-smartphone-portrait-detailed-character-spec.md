## Prompt

```
A realistic low-light smartphone restaurant portrait of a young adult woman sitting inside a warm cafe or restaurant at night. Use a close half-body composition, close to a 4:5 or slightly square-cropped phone photo ratio. The camera is around chest-to-face height, shooting from close range at nearly eye level or slightly above. Her face, shoulders, neck, hand, and upper body fill most of the frame. Do not make it a mirror selfie, do not add a phone covering her face, and do not turn it into a bright studio portrait.

She faces the camera with a slight body turn, holding a metal fork vertically near her chin and lips, as if she paused during dinner while talking or before taking another bite. Her eyes look toward the camera with a calm, soft gaze and a slight sense of curiosity or pause. Her lips are slightly parted or naturally relaxed, with no exaggerated smile.

She has deep black-brown to natural black hair, loosely tied up or half-up, with natural volume at the crown and loose face-framing strands on both sides. The hair has a soft dark sheen under warm low light, with slightly blurred edges. Loose strands rest near the cheeks and neck, adding casual restaurant realism. Preserve her soft oval face, delicate features, large eyes, natural nose bridge, and gentle lip shape. Makeup follows a warm East Asian-inspired restaurant selfie style: translucent base, natural straight brows, thin eyeliner, soft under-eye detail, light pink blush, and rose-pink or muted bean-pink lips.

She wears a white thin-strap camisole top with lace trim, a small bow at the neckline, and tiny floral or dotted details, layered with a warm cream or ivory loose knit cardigan. The cardigan has visible vertical or open-knit texture and slips slightly off the shoulder, revealing the thin strap.

Her skin tone is light warm ivory to soft beige-apricot, appearing creamy and warm under yellow restaurant lighting. The skin on the face, shoulders, neck, and collarbone should look delicate, soft, and slightly translucent, feeling warm, clean, and lightly moisturized to the touch. Keep subtle low-light tonal shifts, soft pores, and natural shadows; avoid excessive smoothing or plastic skin.

The background is a nighttime restaurant, cafe, or indoor dining space decorated with plants. Include dense dark green indoor plants, warm yellow background lights, wooden partitions or window-like frames, softly blurred restaurant seating, dark wall surfaces, and subtle woven chair texture.
```

## 备注

这是目前见到的最详细的单角色描述 prompt 之一，超过 500 词。它展示了"文本 DNA"的极限：(1) 头发——颜色、发型、光泽、松散 strands 的具体位置；(2) 面部——脸型、五官、妆容的每个细节（translucent base, natural straight brows, thin eyeliner...）；(3) 服装——材质（thin-strap camisole with lace trim）、层次（cardigan slips slightly off the shoulder）；(4) 肤色——"light warm ivory to soft beige-apricot"，配合光线条件描述。如果将这种详细程度应用于 DNA Template 中的角色定义，应该能显著提高角色一致性。
