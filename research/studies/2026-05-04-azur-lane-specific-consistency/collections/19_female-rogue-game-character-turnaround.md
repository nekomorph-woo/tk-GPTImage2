## Prompt

```
A game-ready character turnaround sheet of a female rogue character in T-pose, 3:2 aspect ratio, neutral gray background. Show front, three-quarter, and back views. Identity anchors: elven female, early 20s, silver-white short hair with undercut, icy blue eyes, pointed ears, athletic slender build. Dark leather armor with silver buckles, hooded cloak in deep forest green, fingerless gloves, knee-high boots with hidden daggers. Quiver of arrows strapped to back. Consistent proportions, exact same face and body across all views, realistic lighting with subtle rim highlights, high detail texture on leather and metal, professional game character concept art style.
```

## 备注

Dreamina 教程中的游戏角色 T-pose 转角度模板。对碧蓝航线角色的直接启示：

1. **"Identity anchors"概念**：将角色的固定描述词用"Identity anchors:"前缀标记——碧蓝航线角色应该采用类似格式，如"Identity anchors: silver-white long hair with ahoge, gray-blue sharp eyes, cat ears headband, naval officer's jacket, carrier rigging on back"
2. **"exact same face and body across all views"**——这是跨视角一致性的核心声明
3. **武器/装备描述**：原 prompt 的"Quiver of arrows strapped to back"——碧蓝航线角色应该替换为舰装描述
4. **"high detail texture on leather and metal"**——碧蓝航线角色同时包含皮革（军装）和金属（舰装、肩章、武器），这个纹理声明可以直接复用
5. **T-pose vs A-pose 选择**：T-pose 适合展示舰装的全貌（手臂展开时背部的飞行甲板结构更清晰），A-pose 更自然但可能遮挡部分舰装细节
