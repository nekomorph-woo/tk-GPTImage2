## Prompt

```
4:5 vertical poster, ultra high resolution, 8K, luxury fashion campaign, editorial + commercial hybrid, print-ready sharpness

CORE IDEA:
A bold, disruptive fashion statement where movement is exaggerated and dominant, but still feels controlled, raw, and premium.

SCENE / ENVIRONMENT:
Industrial warehouse loft. Exposed aged terracotta brick walls, large factory windows with patinaed brass frames (blurry, cinematic background). Distressed concrete floor with cracks. Warm-toned, diffused natural light filtering in.

SUBJECT:
Female fashion model in customized Miu Miu utility streetwear.
Outfit: Oversized cropped technical jacket in deep muted rust-red, extremely baggy multi-pocket cargo pants in dusty olive green, platform Miu Miu loafers in warm textured caramel-brown leather, opaque cream socks.
Pose: Mid-stride dynamic jump motion, one platform loafer extremely close to camera (hero perspective), body twisting dynamically, hair flying, confident smirk.

PRODUCT FOCUS:
Platform loafer dominates foreground. Ultra detailed textures (chunky leather, shiny buckles in brass, heavy wear, reflections). Glossy + matte material contrast. Perspective distortion exaggerates size.

TYPOGRAPHY:
Primary Headline: "RULES / ARE / OBSOLETE." Right-aligned stacked text, aggressive block sans-serif, urban stencil texture. Warm cream for main text, accent word "OBSOLETE." in bold painted deep crimson brush style.
Secondary Text: "ENGINEERING DISSIDENCE. NOT CONFORMITY." Bottom-right. Small icon row: Built to Last / Rules Don't Apply / Create Danger / Paris France.

BRANDING:
MIU MIU top-right and bottom-left. AW24 Collection. Bottom-right: miumiu.

COLOR GRADING:
Warm, earthy urban palette. Soft shadows + deep crimsons/rusts. Warm highlights from window light. Slight cinematic tone.

LIGHTING:
Natural window daylight. Hard shadows for depth. Specular highlights on leather and buckles.

CAMERA:
Ultra wide-angle (18–24mm). Very low angle (ground level). Forced perspective exaggeration. Foreground sharp, background slightly blurred.

TEXTURE / FINISH:
Subtle grain (2–3%). Slight sharpening on product. Micro contrast boost. Clean but not overly polished.

DESIGN INTENT:
Feels like Y-3 utility + Miu Miu playfulness hybrid. Bold enough for social media. Premium enough for billboard. Immediate scroll-stopping impact.
```
