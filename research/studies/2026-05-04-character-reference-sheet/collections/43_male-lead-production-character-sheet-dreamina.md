# 43 男主制作角色表（3 视角转角度）— Dreamina 模板

- **来源**: [Dreamina](https://dreamina.capcut.com/resource/gpt-image-2-for-character-turnaround-sheets)
- **来源类型**: 博客示例
- **可信度**: 二手来源
- **素材类型**: prompt
- **采集时间**: 2026-05-04

## Prompt

```
A production-ready character turnaround sheet of a male lead character, 3:2 aspect ratio, clean white studio background. Show the character from front view, side view (profile), and back view, standing in a neutral relaxed pose. Character: young East-Asian male, mid-20s, sharp jawline, short dark hair swept to one side, dark brown eyes. Wearing a fitted dark navy blazer over a white crew-neck t-shirt, slim black trousers, polished dark brown leather shoes. Subtle silver watch on left wrist. Cinematic studio lighting with soft shadows, hyper-detailed, professional model sheet quality, consistent proportions across all views.
```

## 备注

Dreamina 教程中的第 1 套模板——男主制作角色表。3 视角（front/side/back）+ 中性姿势 + 纯白背景 + 精确角色描述（年龄/脸型/发型/瞳色/服装/配饰）。3:2 横版比例是转角度表的推荐画幅。
