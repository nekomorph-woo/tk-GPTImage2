## Prompt

```
Create a kawaii-style food photography scene featuring [food item]. Include a small chibi anime character chef with round cheeks, oversized chef hat, tiny apron with heart pattern, holding a wooden spoon. The character should have large sparkling eyes, small nose, rosy cheeks, and a happy expression. Food arranged on a pastel-colored ceramic plate with small decorative elements (tiny flowers, stars sprinkles). Soft pastel background with subtle bokeh. Kawaii aesthetic with warm, inviting lighting. Consistent character proportions — slightly oversized head (1:3 head-to-body ratio), stubby limbs. No text, no watermark.
```

## 备注

Kawaii 风格角色的纯文本 DNA。关键技巧：(1) 比例锁定——"slightly oversized head (1:3 head-to-body ratio), stubby limbs"提供了精确的比例规格；(2) 逐项面部特征描述——"round cheeks, oversized chef hat, large sparkling eyes, small nose, rosy cheeks"；(3) 表情基调——"happy expression"；(4) 风格锁定——"Kawaii aesthetic with warm, inviting lighting"。(1:3 head-to-body ratio) 这种精确比例声明值得借鉴——它比 "cute proportions" 更精确，能显著减少角色比例漂移。
