## Prompt

```
A cinematic character poster in 3:4 vertical format. The character [character description] is rendered in an ink-wash aesthetic with Eastern artistic influences. The composition uses a double-exposure technique — the character's silhouette contains layered scenes of [environmental narrative elements]. Edges are treated with ink diffusion and soft blurring. Large areas of negative space create a balance between presence and absence. Color palette is restrained: [2-3 dominant colors] with subtle accent tones. The overall style is cohesive, refined, and restrained, emphasizing depth, storytelling, and a consistent series-style poster language. ```
```

## 备注

东方美学角色海报模板。一致性策略：(1) "ink-wash aesthetic with Eastern artistic influences"——单一强风格锚点；(2) "a consistent series-style poster language"——系列一致性声明；(3) "Crisp line art where the character is in focus, dissolving into atmospheric wash at the edges"——精确的渲染描述确保风格稳定；(4) "Color palette is restrained"——限制色彩范围减少漂移。该模板适用于同一角色在不同叙事场景中的系列海报。
