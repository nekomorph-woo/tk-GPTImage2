## Prompt

```
Create a children's book illustration page showing [scene description]. Hand-drawn watercolor illustration style with soft washes, gentle color transitions, and visible brushstrokes. Warm earthy color palette with muted greens, soft browns, and gentle yellows. Characters should have slightly oversized heads and expressive faces. White background with soft vignette edges. No text, no watermark.
```

## 备注

展示了"艺术风格作为一致性锚点"的策略。虽然该 prompt 没有定义具体角色，但通过精确的风格描述（"Hand-drawn watercolor illustration style with soft washes, gentle color transitions, and visible brushstrokes. Warm earthy color palette"）确保了跨场景的视觉一致性。在动漫角色一致性中，相同的原理适用：风格声明（如 "soft anime watercolor style" / "clean digital line art"）作为全局一致性锚点。
