# 32 ImagineArt 漫画面板一致性格式模板

- **来源类型**: 博客指南
- **可信度**: 二手来源
- **素材类型**: prompt
- **采集时间**: 2026-05-04

## Prompt

```
A three-panel comic strip. Panel 1: A small robot sits alone at a cafe table with a coffee cup, looking out a rainy window. Panel 2: A stray cat jumps onto the table and knocks over the cup. Panel 3: The robot and the cat stare at each other in surprise. Clean line art, warm color palette, expressive characters.
```

```
A single illustrated story panel: A young explorer in a worn khaki jacket stands at the entrance of a glowing cave, torch in hand, looking in with wonder. Dense jungle behind her. Dramatic light from inside the cave. Adventure comic book aesthetic.
```

```
A two-panel comic. Panel 1: A scientist presents a complex equation on a whiteboard to a confused-looking audience. Panel 2: Close-up on one audience member — a dog in glasses — nodding seriously. Deadpan humor, clean illustration style, simple backgrounds.
```

## 备注

ImagineArt 提供的三个漫画/narrative prompt 示例。一致性策略：(1) 每个 prompt 都在开头声明格式（"three-panel comic strip" / "single illustrated story panel" / "two-panel comic"）；(2) 每个面板都有独立的角色和场景描述；(3) 风格锚点简洁但精确（"Clean line art, warm color palette" / "Adventure comic book aesthetic"）。这些示例展示了：即使没有显式的"Character Consistency"声明，通过为每个面板提供完整的场景描述也能实现一定程度的一致性。
