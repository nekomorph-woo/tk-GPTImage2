## Prompt

（基于 Breaking Bad 剧集 12 个角色的网格海报。每个角色通过独特的服装、姿态和表情来区分。）

```
Create a 4x3 grid poster featuring 12 characters from Breaking Bad. Each character in their own panel with a unique color background that matches their personality. Characters should be in their iconic poses and outfits from the show. Consistent illustration style across all panels — stylized digital art with bold outlines and vibrant colors. No text overlay. Clean grid layout with minimal spacing.
```

## 备注

多角色一致性与区分的双重策略：(1) "Consistent illustration style across all panels"——所有面板共享相同的艺术风格；(2) 每个角色通过"iconic poses and outfits"自然区分；(3) "unique color background that matches their personality"——用背景色强化角色身份。对动漫角色一致性的启示：在多角色场景中，一致性不仅指角色自身的稳定性，还包括跨角色的风格统一性。
