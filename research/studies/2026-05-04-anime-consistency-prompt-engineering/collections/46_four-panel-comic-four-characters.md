# 46 四格漫画四角色（角色区分与一致性并存）

## Prompt

```
Create a four-panel anime comic strip. Each panel features a different character, but all characters share the same anime art style and color palette.

Character 1: A tall male with spiky black hair and red eyes, wearing a dark school uniform, confident smirk.
Character 2: A petite girl with twin braids and golden eyes, wearing a white sailor uniform, shy expression.
Character 3: A muscular male with a buzz cut and scar across his nose, wearing a sports jacket, intense glare.
Character 4: A slender girl with long flowing blue hair and violet eyes, wearing a witch hat and robes, mysterious smile.

Panel 1: Character 1 standing in a school hallway, hands in pockets. Panel 2: Character 2 reading a book in a library. Panel 3: Character 3 on a rooftop, wind blowing. Panel 4: Character 4 in a magical forest, casting a spell.

All panels: Clean anime line art, consistent color grading (cool blue shadows, warm highlights), no text, no watermark. Unified 4-panel grid layout.
```

## 备注

四角色漫画中的区分与一致性并存策略：(1) 每个角色有唯一的外貌标识（头发/眼睛/服装/表情）形成文本 DNA；(2) 所有角色共享统一的艺术风格（"same anime art style and color palette"）；(3) 全局一致性约束（"consistent color grading" + "Unified 4-panel grid layout"）。对动漫角色一致性的核心启示：区分度来源于独特的视觉特征组合（如 "spiky black hair + red eyes + dark school uniform"），而非单个特征。
